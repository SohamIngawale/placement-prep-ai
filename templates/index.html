About PlacePrep

Our mission is to help every student land their dream tech job.

Who We Are

PlacePrep was built by industry professionals and former placement coordinators who understand the anxiety and sheer volume of preparation required to crack top tier tech interviews. We believe that with the right guidance, curated resources, and consistent practice, anyone can break into top product and service companies.

What We Do

  • Curate highly accurate previous year questions (PYQs) from top companies.
  • Provide AI-driven mock interviews and resume reviews.
  • Build personalized roadmaps based on your current skill level.

Contact Us

Have questions or feedback? Reach out to us at support@placeprep.ai

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create or modify your account, use our interactive mock interviews, or submit your resume for AI review. This includes your username, email, and any content you upload.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including delivering personalized roadmaps, tracking your progress, and communicating with you about your account.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse and unauthorized access. Your resumes and interview data are securely processed and never sold to third parties.
